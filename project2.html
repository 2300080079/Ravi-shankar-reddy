simple recipe: chocolate chip cookies

ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoon hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts(optional)

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees c).
  2. cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar untill smooth.
  3. beat in the eggs one ata time, then stir in the vennila
  4. drop the large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  5. bake for 10 minutes in the preheated oven , or untill edges are nicely browned.
